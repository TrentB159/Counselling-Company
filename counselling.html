Counselling Services


During counselling the client and therapist work collaboratively in an atmosphere that is compassionate, supportive, and confidential. Therapeutic conversations allow clients to gain understanding and insight, while fostering the development of new skills to manage personal, family and social challenges.


  • Explore behaviour, relationships, feelings or thoughts that trouble you or cause difficulty in your life
  • Gain support and access resources to help manage a crisis or difficult time
  • Adopt new and effective strategies and techniques to better understand and cope with problems
  • Develop deeper personal insight and awareness
  • Develop more fulfilling relationships and learn to communicate more effectively with others
  • Improve relationship dynamics and create more harmony within your family
  • Improve self-esteem
  • Have a greater sense of enjoyment, satisfaction and meaning in your life
