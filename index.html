Counselling and Company was founded with the ideals of healing, compassion, and resilience. When receiving counselling services from Maddy Battisti, you can expect to be welcomed into a safe space, with respect and understanding.

Counselling and Company’s mission statement is You Possess all the tools necessary for change, let’s discover them, due to the belief that all individuals have a unique set of strengths that can be a facilitator and driving force of positive change.
