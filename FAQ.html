Below are some frequently asked question that you might find helpful. If you have any other questions regarding me or my counselling services feel free to reach out through the form below.

  • What services do you offer?
    I will be offering individual and *eventually* couple and group counselling services. Individuals are able to book either 50 minute or 90 minute sessions depending on their individual needs.
    Do you have a speciality?
    I have worked with young adults and youth in the past. I use a person-centred narrative approach in my work.
    Why did you choose counselling as a career?
    I have always loved listening to others and working in union to find solutions. I wanted to work at a job where no day was ever the same.
    What do you love most about your career in counselling?
    During my practicum and into the early stages of my areer I am humbled by how willing clients have been to share intimate details about their life. I hold the confidentiality of clients in high standerd in my practice.
    What audience are you catering to?
    Starting out my career I am wanting and willing to cater to anyone wishing to obtain counselling services. I have worked closely with young adults and children during my practicum. However, wish to see all ages (and stages) in the work that I do.
    How long are your sessions?
    I am offering a free 20 minute "intro" session. This is for potential clients to get to know me, the process, and make the best decision for themselves if they'd like to work with me. Individual session will be 50 and 90 minutes thereafter.
    What are you most excited about now that you're beginning you practice?
    I am excited to start building connection with clients, and being able to use what I've learned in my Masters program and practicum experience to help others.
